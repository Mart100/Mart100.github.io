How to connect your own Challonge account to Toorney and login



1: First, Go to the Challonge website: https://challonge.com/





2: Click on your profile in the top right corner, and click settings

3: Click on "Developer API"

4: Copy your "API key"

5: Paste your "API key" back to the toorney bot in DM
