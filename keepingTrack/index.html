Frequently Asked Questions:
What is Keeping Track?
Keep track of your daily life and analyze to gain insight of your habits! A lot of things happen in your life! And sometimes its hard to keep track of your habits. In this app you can report daily parameters of your day. Such as bed time, food quality or the amount of minutes you jogged. Its easy to forget what you did each day, but if you keep every day of your life stored in an app, you can easier see trends or good habits in your daily life and what causes them.
